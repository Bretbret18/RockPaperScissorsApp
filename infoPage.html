Rock, Paper, Scissors! Game Instructions

Game
Welcome to Rock, Paper, Scissors!
The objective of the game is to chose either Rock, Paper, or Scissors and defeat the opponent in a best out of 3 match! In case you are not familiar with the rules of this game:
Rock beats Scissors
Scissors beats Paper
Paper beats Rock
If you chose the same button as the opponent, the result of the round will be a draw. Draw results are of no value to the player or the opponent; Either the player or the opponent must win the match by winning 2 rounds.
1. Choose a button (rock, paper, or scissors)
2. Press the Shoot! button
3. Wait until the final results of the round are displayed.
4. Both the players symbol as well as the opponents symbol will light up as this red color when the round is complete. Once the round is complete, click the next round button!
5. The game will carry on in this manner until either you the player has won 2 rounds or the opponent has won 2 rounds.
6. Once the match is over, feel free to play again!
Good Luck!
