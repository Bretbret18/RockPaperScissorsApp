Game Info

Game
  • RULES OF GAME:
    • Rock beats Scissors
    Scissors beats Paper
    Paper beats Rock
  • OBJECTIVE:
    • Beat the opponent in a best out of three match!
  • DIRECTIONS:
    • Choose Rock, Paper, or Scissors
    Click on 'Shoot!' button
    Wait for 'Shoot!' event to finish
    Click 'Next Round' button to go to next round
    Play until either you or opponent wins best out of 3!
    Click 'Play Again' button to restart game
